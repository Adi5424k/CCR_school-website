About Us

SOCSE (School of Computer Science and Engineering) is dedicated to providing quality education in the field of computer science. Our goal is to equip students with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in the tech industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a learning environment where students are encouraged to explore, innovate, and create. We aim to provide a comprehensive education that balances theoretical knowledge with practical application.

Our Vision

We envision a world where SOCSE graduates lead the way in technological advancements, contributing to society through their skills and knowledge.

History

SOCSE was established in 2020 with a vision to become a leader in computer science education. Over the years, we have grown into a respected institution with a diverse student body and a commitment to excellence.
